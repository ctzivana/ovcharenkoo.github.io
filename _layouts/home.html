I am a PhD Candidate with entrepreneural mindset at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) in Seismic Modeling and Inversion group run by Prof. Daniel Peter. I am broadly interested in numerical methods in global and exploration geophysics, which includes but not limited to:

  • Seismic Imaging
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural stress state reconstruction
  • HPC
My PhD research is focused on applying ML techniques to geophysical problems.

Bio

I grew up in Moscow and received my M.Sc degree in Earth Science from Lomonosov Moscow State University in 2014. During the M.Sc program my research was focused on natural stress-state reconstruction. I was working under the supervision of Dr. Yu.L. Rebetsky in the tectonophysics lab at the Schmidt Institute of Physics of the Earth of the Russian Academy of Sciences.

In 2015 I got International Master of Research in Exploration Geophysics (GPX) degree from the Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris (IPGP, Paris VII) in Paris. There, I worked with Prof. Nobuaki Fuji and Dr. Roland Martin (CNRS) on accurate finite difference operators for synthetic seismogram calculation in areas with sharp lithological interfaces.

Aside research activities I like learning languages, good food, BJJ, playing golf and guitar.
